About Justin Farr & Business Technology Advantages

Hands-on IT consulting expertise rooted in Auburn, AL — serving clients across Alabama, Georgia, and the Southeast.

Our Mission

Business Technology Advantages, LLC exists to bridge the gap between complex technology and practical business outcomes. Founded by Justin Farr in Auburn, Alabama, BTA delivers expert IT consulting that helps organizations — from fast-growing startups to established enterprises — harness the full potential of their data and technology infrastructure.

We believe that every business, whether in Opelika, Montgomery, Birmingham, Atlanta, or a small town in between, deserves access to the same caliber of technology consulting that drives Fortune 500 companies. That's the advantage we bring.

Meet Justin Farr

Justin Farr is the founder and principal consultant of Business Technology Advantages, LLC, and a Professor of Practice at the Auburn University College of Business, where he teaches database management and programming. This unique combination of academic rigor and real-world consulting gives Justin a perspective few IT consultants can match.

Justin holds a Master of Science in Information Systems and Decision Sciences and a Bachelor of Science in Information Systems and Decision Sciences, both from Louisiana State University. His career spans over two decades in the technology industry — from network administration and software development to IT management, solutions architecture, and university leadership.

Before founding BTA, Justin served as Director of Information and Instructional Technology at Auburn University's College of Veterinary Medicine, where he led technology strategy and operations. Prior to Auburn, he held key roles including Director of Information Services at the LSU Foundation, IT Manager and Solutions Architect at Blue Cross Blue Shield of Louisiana, and IT Manager/Programmer at Stonetrust Commercial Insurance. He also gained early-career experience as a Software Developer at Unisys Corporation.

Based in Auburn, AL, Justin works directly with clients in Opelika, Montgomery, Birmingham, Tuscaloosa, Dothan, Huntsville, Atlanta, Columbus, and beyond. His approach is simple: understand the business problem first, then apply the right technology to solve it — no unnecessary complexity, no bloated scope.
